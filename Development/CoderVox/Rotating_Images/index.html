Home prices are significantly increasing

These home are beautiful. Why aren't they being bought?

A house like this is becoming increasingly difficult fo ryounger people to buy.

By Gray | Senior editor of The News

More and more millennials are not able to afford homes. This is because of rising home prices, a low supply of afforadable housing, as well as many young people choosing to put off marriage.

80 percent of people who do not own homes said they couldn't afford it because of student loans. This means that recent college graduates are focused on paying for their education, which lowers the chances of them becoming homeowners.
